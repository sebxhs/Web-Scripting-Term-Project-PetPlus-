PetPlus was founded in 2020 with a vision: to create a one-stop shop where pet owners could find everything they need for their beloved companions. What started as a small business with a big dream has grown into a trusted name in pet care, serving thousands of happy pets and their owners.

Because we understand that your pet isn’t just a pet—they’re family.
